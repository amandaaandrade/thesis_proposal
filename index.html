Project Description:

Project Overview

2d animation, duration 2-3 mins, maybe. This will be set in the future, let's just say for now, 3000 years. The world has become very industrial; everything from people's homes to factories has an industrial style hell bent on destroying nature, so imagine the same toxic fumes emitted from factories are now emitted from homes. Many are content to live this way, but some are not. For those who are not there is THE MOTH (a magical giant moth) who is the leader of the cult. THE MOTH promises that if you give your flesh to the moth for consumption, you will be transformed into a Moth to fly away from this world and become one with nature. Those who follow the moth are Monks and those who recruit monks are priests. After you have given most of your flesh and are about to die, monks enter a ceremony in pairs of 3 (3 symbolizes birth, life, and death). During the ceremony, the monks walk through the light, and spectators will see a moth come out of the other side; however, in reality, you are dead, there is no escape from reality. This is possibly how priests are created, where they are given the choice of death or recruitment of others to the same fate to continue living longer.

I am unsure of the angle to explore this world yet; however, I hope to pass on a religious critique as well as a sense that you cannot run away from your problems in the present, like climate change, but I hope you can take this and apply it to any problem you are currently running away from.
